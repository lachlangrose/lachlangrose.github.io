add_gradient_ctr_pts(w=1.0)¶

Adds gradient constraints to the least squares system with a weight defined by w :param w - either numpy array of length number of:

Notes

Gradient constraints add a constraint that the gradient of the implicit function should be orthogonal to the strike vector and the dip vector defined by the normal. This does not control the direction of the gradient and therefore requires at least two other value constraints OR a norm constraint for the interpolant to solve.
